Our Vision

UNFOLD e.V. is a youth-led, non-profit organization based in Germany working at the intersection of youth empowerment, mental health, embodiment and peace education.

We create safe, inclusive and experiential learning spaces where young people and youth workers can reconnect — with themselves, others, and the world around them.

We see resilience not as performance, but as the capacity to stay connected.

We aim to contribute to a more compassionate Europe through embodied, relational education.

Transformation begins in the body — in presence and awareness.

Our Team

UNFOLD is carried by a small collective of educators, facilitators and practitioners.

We combine experience from education, therapy, movement, theatre pedagogy, psychology and community work.

What connects us is a shared orientation toward presence, honesty and relational learning.

Our Methodology

Our methodology is rooted in embodied, relational and experiential learning.

We integrate movement, mindfulness, dialogue, creativity and nature-based practices.

We work with emotional awareness, communication and relational safety.

We focus on presence rather than performance.

Education is the creation of conditions for deeper connection — to self, others and the world.
