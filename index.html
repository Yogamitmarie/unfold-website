Our Vision

UNFOLD e.V. works at the intersection of youth empowerment, mental health, embodiment and peace education.

We create spaces for reconnection — with self, others and the world.

We understand resilience as connection, not performance.

Transformation begins in the body.

Our Methodology

We work through embodied, relational and experiential learning.

Movement, mindfulness, dialogue and nature are core elements.

We prioritise presence over performance.

Learning is becoming in relation.
