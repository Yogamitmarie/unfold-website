Our Vision

UNFOLD e.V. is a youth-led, non-profit organization based in Germany working at the intersection of youth empowerment, mental health, embodiment and peace education.

We create safe, inclusive and experiential learning spaces where young people and youth workers can reconnect: with themselves, with others, and with the wider social and natural surroundings they are part of.

We work with the understanding that many young people today grow up in environments shaped by pressure, acceleration and emotional disconnection.

Our vision is to contribute to a more peaceful and compassionate Europe by integrating personal development with civic engagement and intercultural dialogue.

Our Methodology

Our methodology is rooted in embodied, relational and experiential learning.

We integrate movement, mindfulness, creative expression, dialogue and nature-based practices.

Rather than focusing on performance, we support authenticity, awareness and resilience.
