Our Vision

UNFOLD e.V. is a youth-led, non-profit organization based in Germany working at the intersection of youth empowerment, mental health, embodiment and peace education.

We create safe, inclusive and experiential learning spaces where young people and youth workers can reconnect: With themselves, with others, and with the wider social and natural surrounding they are part of.

We work with the understanding that many young people today grow up in environments shaped by pressure to compete, acceleration and emotional disconnection. In response, we see the importance of resilience for Youth in this fast changing world.

Our vision is to contribute to a more peaceful and compassionate Europe (and beyond) by integrating personal development with civic engagement, intercultural dialogue and social responsibility. We believe that transformation begins in the body - in presence, awareness and the ability to feel.

Our Team

UNFOLD is carried by a small collective of educators, facilitators and practitioners with long-term experience in youth work, non-formal education and embodied learning.

What connects us is the shared intention: To create spaces where people can slow down, listen inwardly, and relate to each other in more honest and human ways.

Marie Wächter

Chairwoman of UNFOLD e.V., works as a yoga teacher and body-oriented practitioner with a focus on mental health.

Marenka Sedlaczek

Vice-Chairwoman, works as a pedagogue, primary school teacher and theatre educator.

Our Methodology

Our methodology is rooted in embodied, relational and experiential learning.

We work through non-formal education methods that integrate movement, mindfulness, creative expression and dialogue.

Our approach is trauma-sensitive and inclusive.
